Visualization Matters.

Here, we are visualized.

In this project, we will use D3 to build stand-alone prototype visualizations of Dell SonicWALL firewall data that could be integrated into their Dell SonicWALL Analyzer product.

Learn more on Github

This project is for CS690 Master Project course during the semester fall 2014. Sponsors are going to give the students some chances and guide them to get touch to the real industry projects.
