Markdown notes:

italic:
_this_

Header:
#header1
##header2 
….. 
######header6


Hyperlink:
create a hyperlink to www.github.com, with a link text that says, Visit GitHub!, you'd write this in Markdown: [Visit GitHub!](www.github.com)
Create links with a pic :
![pic](www.github.com)

Block quote: (special attention):
> this……..


List:
unordered list: 
	*  list1   (a space between * and list1)
numbered list :
1.  list1
2.  list2

Break:
hard break (insert a new line)
soft break : insert two space after each new line :
Do I contradict myself?··
Very well then I contradict myself,··
(I am large, I contain multitudes.)
the dot is a space.
